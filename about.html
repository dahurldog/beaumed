About Us

Serving our community for over 50 years — accredited, trusted and deeply rooted in Beaudesert and the Scenic Rim.

"70+ years dedicated to Beaudesert and rural Queensland — woven into the community we proudly call home."

Dual College Accredited — RACGP & ACRRM

Beaudesert Medical Centre holds the rare distinction of being accredited by both the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners (RACGP) and the Australian College of Rural and Remote Medicine (ACRRM) — the two peak GP colleges in Australia.

We are a proud clinical teaching practice for the University of Queensland and Griffith University, and a post-graduate training provider through General Practice Training Queensland across both RACGP and ACRRM pathways.

Our commitment to education means our doctors stay at the forefront of evidence-based practice — and that's good news for every patient we see.

Our Story

Serving Our Community for Over 50 Years

Beaudesert Medical Centre was established on our present site in the mid-1970s, in a distinctive building that provides a "one-stop shop" for your health. We are a traditional family general practice — comprehensive, caring and deeply connected to the people of Beaudesert and the Scenic Rim.

We have seen many doctors and nurses through our rooms but we still believe in good old-fashioned long-term care for you and your family. Our distinctive building was extended in 2009 to make more space for us and other long-term providers — including pharmacy, pathology, optometry and dentist.

Whether you need a routine check-up, management of a long-term condition or something more urgent, our team is here to provide the highest standard of care in a welcoming, unhurried environment.

Hospital Visits
