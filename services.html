Specialist Areas

Special Services

In addition to general consultations, our team offers a broad range of targeted services for specific health needs.

🍼

Babies & Children

Vaccinations and checkups follow the "Red Book" schedule at 6 weeks, 4, 6, 12 and 18 months and 4 years. Developmental assessments available at any stage.

👩

Women's Health

Full antenatal service including pregnancy checkups, tests and scans. Private maternity options: Sunnybank Private, Greenslopes Private and Pindara Private.

  • Contraception — pill, barrier methods, LARCs
  • Mirena, Implanon, Depo-Provera
  • Cervical screening (Pap/HPV)
👨

Men's Health

Men often delay seeking help — our GPs actively address the health challenges that affect men most:

  • Heart disease & cancer screening
  • Anxiety, depression & suicide risk
  • Sleep apnoea & erectile dysfunction
  • Skin cancer checks
😴

Tired All the Time?

Persistent fatigue deserves a thorough investigation. We offer a comprehensive evaluation including:

  • Blood tests & x-rays
  • Sleep apnoea testing
  • ECG & exercise stress testing
☀️

Skin Cancer

Full head-to-toe skin assessments with a complete range of treatments:

  • Cryotherapy (freezing)
  • Anticancer creams (Efudix, Aldara, Picato)
  • Minor surgery & curettage and cautery
  • Referrals for plastic surgery if needed
💉

Diabetes

3–4 bulk-billed annual checkups monitoring blood pressure, weight, heart risk, eyes, kidneys and feet. Funded allied health referrals available for eligible patients.

❤️

Heart Disease

Cardiovascular risk assessment and long-term management. 3–4 bulk-billed annual checkups for patients post-heart attack, stent or bypass surgery.

🫁

Lung Disease

Assessment and management of asthma, emphysema, chronic bronchitis and lung cancer. Spirometry testing available. 3–4 bulk-billed annual checkups for eligible patients.

📋

Chronic Disease Management

3–4 bulk-billed checkups annually under GP Management Plans for: heart disease, stroke, lung disease, diabetes, arthritis and child developmental delays.

🧠

Mental Health

Mental Health Treatment Plans with psychology referrals. ATAPS and Better Outcomes programs offer fully-funded psychology through Brisbane South PHN.

🌿

ATSI Health

Dedicated Medicare programs for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander patients:

  • 9–12 monthly health checks
  • 4–6 monthly Chronic Disease Management Plans
  • Closing the Gap prescriptions
  • Tailored immunisation programs
Medicare ATSI information line: 1800 556 955
👴

Over-75 Health Checks

Bulk-billed annual health assessments for patients aged 75 and over, often combined with driver's licence medical examinations.

✈️

Travel Medicine

Comprehensive pre-travel advice — book at least six weeks before overseas departure with your itinerary and vaccination history. We'll help with:

  • Vaccinations & medications
  • myHealthRecord setup
  • Scripts & medicine planning
  • Emergency contacts & GPS apps
🦺

WorkCover

Work-related injury and illness claims management, coordinated with allied health and specialist consultations. WorkCover appointments are bulk-billed.

🐄

Q Fever Immunisation

Essential for anyone working with cattle, sheep, goats or other animals. Requires two visits seven days apart — skin and blood tests first, then immunisation if both are negative. Immunity develops 15 days after the second visit. Special pricing applies — ask reception. Employer enquiries: manager@beaumed.com.au

Medications & Results

Prescriptions & Test Results

A few simple steps make sure you're never caught without your medicine or left wondering about your results.

💊 Prescriptions

Usually when long-term medicine is running low, it's time for a checkup. The best time to book is when you fill your last repeat script — that way you have plenty of choices about when to be seen.

Sometimes it's OK to get a script without a checkup, especially if you've been in recently for the same condition. Phone us and ask — always call at least 2 days before your medicine runs out (a week is best).

During the summer storm and flood season, it's a good idea to keep at least a fortnight's worth of medicines spare at home, in case you're cut off from easy access to a doctor or pharmacy.

🔬 Test Results

When your doctor arranges any tests or investigations, they will make a follow-up plan which you must agree to follow — for your own health and safety.

Usually you will have an appointment to check how you are going, review your results and plan the next steps. If the plan is for you to phone us, make sure you do.

⚠️ No news is not the same as good news. Calling BMC is the best way to make sure your results have returned and have been seen by your doctor.
