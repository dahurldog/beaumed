Getting Here

Parking & Transport

47 William Street, Beaudesert — centrally located with easy access and ample parking.

🅿️

Parking

Ample parking available in William Street, Albert Street and Duckett Street. Wheelchair access is available and our ambulance entrance in Duckett Street is available if your mobility problems are severe.

🚌

Public Transport

Translink bus 540 stops in Anna Street near the ANZ Bank — approximately 5 minutes walk away. The bus runs hourly along Mt Lindesay Highway to and from Browns Plains.

🚕

Taxi

Taxis are available on (07) 3249 6319 or 131 008.

🤝

Subsidised Transport

Subsidised transport services may be available for you, including Centacare, Able Australia and the RSL minibus. Speak to our reception team for more information.
