Medicare

Chronic Disease Plans & Care Arrangements

Understanding your Medicare entitlements can significantly reduce the cost of your long-term healthcare.

GP Management Plan (GPMP)

If you have a condition that will last more than 6 months, or is terminal, you may be eligible for a GP Management Plan. Regular checkups every 3–6 months are bulk-billed — Medicare pays better rebates for keeping your health well-managed.

Team Care Arrangement (TCA)

If you need treatment from 2 or more health professionals, a Team Care Arrangement coordinates your care and unlocks Medicare rebates for up to 5 allied health visits per year — including physio, dietetics, podiatry and psychology.

Registering Your Bank Account

To receive Medicare rebates directly to your bank account, call 132 011 (24 hrs), visit a Medicare service centre, or register via myGov or the Express Plus Medicare app.
