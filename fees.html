Fees & Billing

We aim to make healthcare as accessible as possible. Many services are bulk-billed, and we offer pensioner discounts. If you're unsure, just ask.

✅ Bulk-Billed Services

BMC bulk-bills the following services — meaning no out-of-pocket cost to you for these appointments:

  • Children's scheduled immunisations
  • WorkCover appointments
  • Veteran's Affairs (DVA) appointments
  • Most GP Management Plans (GPMP)
  • Team Care Arrangements (TCA)
  • Mental Health Treatment Plans
  • 3–4 monthly chronic disease checkups (for eligible patients on plans)
  • Over-75 health checks
  • ATSI health checks and chronic disease management

Medicare

Medicare & Chronic Disease Plans

Understanding your Medicare entitlements can significantly reduce the cost of your healthcare.

What is a GP Management Plan?

If you have a disease or condition that will be present for more than 6 months, or that is terminal, you may be eligible for a Chronic Disease Plan (GP Management Plan).

Patients on plans can have a regular checkup every 3–6 months, and BMC bulk-bills for these because Medicare pays better rebates for staying well-organised.

If you need treatment from 2 or more health professionals, you may be eligible for a Team Care Arrangement (TCA). This involves a regular checkup every 3–6 months and offers better Medicare rebates when your care is coordinated.

If you are on a Team Care Arrangement, Medicare rebates for up to 5 allied health visits per year are available to you — including physiotherapy, dietetics, podiatry and psychology. Your doctor or nurse will authorise these at your regular checkup.

To register your bank account details with Medicare for direct rebate payments, call the Medicare general enquiries line on 132 011 (24 hours a day), or visit a service centre, or register through myGov / Express Plus Medicare app.
